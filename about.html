About Brainiacs

Know more about Brainiacs

Brainiacs TLC was founded by an OCT Certified Teacher, and experienced tutoring professional, with vast experience consulting with families, identifying and reporting students’ strengths, progress and academic next steps. Using her knowledge of the Ontario Curriculum, she formulates learning / growth plans with achievable steps, in order for clients to see and feel success.

Currently a Reading Recovery teacher; She is a Certified Reading Specialist, as well as Additional Qualification courses in ESL, Mathematics, and Computers. Furthermore, she has worked as an Ontario Focused Intervention Partnership (OFIP) tutor, providing intensive support to children identified as being below grade standard. At Brainiacs, she is responsible for developing individualized programming for our students, based on comprehensive assessments that she performs with them. She follows that up by establishing and overseeing individualized program goals, and the communication of progress to the family. Her insight and expertise are key tools in selecting and training Brainiacs’ Program Specialists.
